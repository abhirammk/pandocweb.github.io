Short Bio

My colleagues will classify me as either a mathematician or a mathematical physicist with an inability to be happy unless things are computed explicitly. Personally, I view myself as a fool trying to make sense of things.

I love computing and solving all kinds of mathematical problems. I also develop mathematical software and algorithms.

I am a member of the Mathematical Structures in Physics group and an affiliate member of the Numerical Algebraic Geometry Group at the Max Planck Institute for Mathematics in the Sciences.
